About the Mortgage Payment Calculator

This calculator estimates your full monthly mortgage payment — principal and interest, property tax, homeowners insurance, and PMI when applicable. The total is commonly called PITI.

What this calculator does not include

  • HOA fees or community assessments
  • Closing costs (typically 2-5% of loan amount)
  • Mortgage interest tax deduction
  • FHA or VA loan-specific insurance structures
  • Adjustable-rate mortgage (ARM) features

How to use it

Try different down payment amounts to see the impact on PMI — reaching 20% equity removes the PMI line entirely. Also compare 15-year vs. 30-year terms: the 15-year payment is higher, but total interest paid is dramatically lower.
