Our Investment Philosophy

  • Low-cost ETF and index fund core for broad market exposure
  • Selective satellite positions in active strategies, real assets, or alternatives when they earn their fees
  • Asset location: putting tax-inefficient holdings in IRAs and tax-efficient holdings in taxable accounts
  • Annual tax-loss harvesting in taxable accounts to reduce your tax bill while keeping market exposure
  • Rebalancing on tolerance bands, not the calendar — we act when markets demand it

What We Don't Do

  • We don't pick individual stocks for clients or chase the latest hot fund
  • We don't trade frequently or charge commissions on any transaction
  • We don't promise to beat the market — we promise to capture market returns at low cost with disciplined risk management

Frequently Asked Questions

What is asset allocation?

Asset allocation is how you split your portfolio across stocks, bonds, real estate, and other asset classes. Research suggests this single decision drives most of your long-term return and volatility.

How often do you rebalance?

We rebalance based on drift tolerance bands — typically when a position is 20% off its target weight — rather than rigid quarterly schedules. This captures market volatility more efficiently.

Do you offer socially responsible or ESG investing?

Yes. We can build portfolios screened for ESG, faith-based values, or mission alignment using diversified, low-cost ETFs.
